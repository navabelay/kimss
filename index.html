I've been in buisness for 20 years.

Hello there, my name is Kimberli Johnson and welcome to Kim’s Savory Kuisines, LLC!

I have always loved to cook. My specialty is “Soul Food,” but I am also extremely talented in all Cuisines (Mexican, Asian, Italian, American Creole/Cajun) I remember whipping up meals for me and my sister at an early age, asking my grandparents how to cook something before and after living on my own. I have received recipes from both sides of my family and have enhanced the recipes with my own techniques; however, the foundation has remained the same. I also have passed down my knowledge and recipes to my children and grandchildren. We are all confident in what we bring to the TABLE and that you will enjoy every “CRUMB.”
